 Description of observable trends based on the data 
Observation 1: 
Charter schools are public schools that are independent of school districts through contracts with 
state or local boards and District schools are tied to school districts and set their curriculums
based on state education standards From the above results, we can observe that the charter schools 
are small in size as compare to district school. The overall performance is very high in charter schools. 
Observation 2: 
Schools with low budget per student performed better than the high budget per student. 
Observation 3:
In general, students performed better in reading than the math. The passing percentage in reading
is high than the math percentage.
